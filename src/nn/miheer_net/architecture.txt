Cards (grid: [B,4,4,13]) --> CardsEncoder (CNN w/ residual blocks, global pool, proj) -----\
                                                                                             \
Bets (hand_log: [B,64,8] or flat [B,512]) --> BetsTransformer (pos enc + encoder + pooling) --> Fusion trunk (MLP) --> Policy logits + Bet mean/std + Next hand/game state
                                                                                             /
Hand/Game internal state ([B,H],[B,G]) --> StateMLP (dual-branch MLP) ----------------------/

Key notes:
- Three modular branches: CNN for cards, Transformer for betting history, MLP for hand/game state.
- Fusion trunk combines embeddings; heads emit action logits, bet mean/logvar->std, and refreshed internal states.
- Internal states (hand/game) are initialized once, refreshed each forward, and attached back to observations for replay-aware rollouts.
